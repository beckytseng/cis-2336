About me

Hi there, thank you for checking in!

I'm currently majoring in Computer Information Systems. I'm 24 years old and still in college, a bit of a late bloomer. I can't wait to graduate and get my degree, I feel old whenever I'm on campus.

I love cats and plants, I'm extremely introverted. I'm very happy to take online classes so I don't have to commute or interact with too many people. On a regular day you will find me at home drinking a cup of tea, tending my plants, and playing with my cats. I have two black cats named Zoe and Caesar, they are both rescues.

Before I took this class, I have built a personal website for myself to showcase my baking creations and for my clients to book me through my website for petsitting services. I am happy to be in this class to learn more.

Computers and technology has always fascinated me, I just didn't think I would major in it. I always joke that Computer Information Systems is the lesser version of Computer Science because I'm not bright enough to do heavy coding courseworks. I love learning to code and to learn the mechanics of it all but I'm not sure I am up for the challenge. Many of my friends majored in Computer Science and I honestly don't think I'm smart enough for it, it takes a lot of practice, dedication, and intelligence.
